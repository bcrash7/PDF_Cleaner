## Communication Methods:

- Sales Team frist, then Patient Liaisons during the time of transition
- Physician Engagement Directors after the practice's commencement date.

Business Listing : Our marketing partners, MDG, manage the practice's Google Business Listings for the doctor, allowing them to participate in Online Review Monitoring. Mail - Casey Dixon - Outlook

Review Solicitation : An email is sent to patients requesting reviews for their physician.

Patient Newsletter : Called 'Healthy Living' this monthly Newsletter goes out to patients of doctors who have opted into this program. Each newsletter has a link to our SignatureMD Blog, as well as other lifestyle, fitness, nutrition articles, plus a link to our monthly perk.

Personalized Collateral Materials : Creation of Appointment Cards, Business Cards, Letterhead, envelopes, Thank You Cards and More.

Referral Program : Physicians are eligible to earn up to $25,000 for referring other physicians to SignatureMD, providing that physician signs up and has a successful launch.
