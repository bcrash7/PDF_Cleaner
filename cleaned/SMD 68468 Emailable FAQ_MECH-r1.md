## Frequently Asked Questions

## What is SignatureMD?

SignatureMD is a concierge medical services company that provides customized turnkey solutions enabling physicians to remain independent and thrive in today's challenging healthcare environment.

## How does the SignatureMD model work?

While SignatureMD customizes its program for each affiliated physician, typically SignatureMD offers a Segmented Practice Model in which patients who desire to continue and enrich their relationship with a trusted physician are given the opportunity to join the personalized care program, while patients who place less emphasis on their healthcare are managed by an associate physician or physician-extender. The SignatureMD segmented model has similar conversion yields to competitors' all-or-nothing conversion models, without requiring physicians to take on unnecessary financial or ethical risk.

SignatureMD offers the only high-reward, low-risk customized concierge model.

## Maximized Income on Average:

Physicians who integrated our personalized care model into their practices in recent years typically increased membership revenue by over $400,000 on average.

Program Flexibility: SignatureMD does not impose its model on you, but rather tailors a program to meet your unique practice characteristics, personal needs, and professional objectives.

## How does SignatureMD get paid?

SignatureMD is only successful when its affiliated physicians are successful. In fact, SignatureMD shoulders all of the economic risk by making a six-figure investment in each practice it converts.

SignatureMD and its affiliated physicians' interests are completely aligned. Affiliated physicians retain the majority of the membership fees.

## My patients are not wealthy, so how will they afford this?

Many current program members are not wealthy and come from all walks of life, including teachers, lawyers, accountants, farmers, and CEOs. The factors that unify these patients are respect and admiration for their physician and placing value on their health.

## Is insurance reimbursement affected?

Patients pay a membership fee for non-covered, non-medical services. SignatureMD members and standard FFS patients will continue to use their insurance or Medicare as they have always done.

## How will my SignatureMD practice become a valuable retirement asset?

While standard FFS practices are sold at severe discounts, a SignatureMD membership-based practice is saleable at a multiple of income. SignatureMD assists retiring physicians in recruiting a replacement physician and acts as a conduit in the sale.

## What do SignatureMD Personalized Care Members receive for their membership?

Program members partner with their physicians to reach optimal health. A customized prevention and wellness plan is the foundation of this partnership. Physicians and members meet at appropriate intervals to gauge progress against their plan. Members, are guaranteed same-day or next-business-day appointments that start on time and last as long as necessary, as well as 24/7 telephone or email availability from their physician. Unlike its competitors, SignatureMD does not dictate its affiliates' delivery of care, but rather provides recommendations and best practices.
