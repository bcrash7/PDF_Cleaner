## Be free to practice medicine the way it was meant to be practiced

Our experienced team makes it easy to convert your practice to a successful concierge medicine model. We take care of every detail and provide long-term business and marketing support. If you'd like to explore how to start a concierge medical practice, review our conversion process below and speak to one of our concierge medicine consultants to evaluate your practice.

## 1-2 weeks

## Practice evaluation

Our concierge medicine consultants and data scientists use a sophisticated proprietary model to analyze your practice and determine whether it will be a good fit for the concierge medicine model. Once we determine that your practice is a strong candidate for concierge medicine, we'll conduct an in-depth patient survey and incorporate our findings into a thorough analysis that illustrates the financial and personal gains you could achieve by working with the SignatureMD team.

## 4-6 weeks

## Conversion preparation

Together, we'll develop a personalized conversion plan that includes staff training, prospective member education, and an integrated marketing campaign to promote your concierge medicine program.

## 1 day

## Patient presentations

We'll host a large-scale, complimentary event and may facilitate smaller presentations with your patients, which takes place virtually.

8-12 weeks

## On-site management

A SignatureMD patient liaison will be on site at your office for 8 to 12 weeks to meet one-on-one with your patients, educate them about the value of your concierge medicine program, and enroll those who are interested.

## Ongoing commitment

As part of our commitment to helping you attain long-term success, our marketing experts will continue to work on your behalf, even after your practice conversion is complete. We'll provide a wide range of online and traditional marketing to promote your practice, as well as the following ongoing support:

- Membership fee billing and collections
- Regulatory support
- Staff training
- Recommendations regarding technology
- Website development and management
- Collateral development
- Online business listings management
- Online reputation management
- Digital advertising
- Social media
