## What is an ACO?

An ACO (Accountable Care Organization) is a network of healthcare providers who voluntarily collaborate to offer coordinated, high-quality care to Medicare patients. ACOs aim to ensure that patients, especially the chronically ill, get the right care at the right time, while avoiding unnecessary duplication of services and preventing medical errors.
