SignatureMD is committed to continually supporting the growth of your practice, allowing you to better engage with your patients and improve the membership experience. Each program is designed to help you encourage positive patient interactions and promote your practice.

## Customized website features

Your personalized website is full of customizable features that allow you to better engage patients, improve search engine optimization (SEO), and help grow your practice. Each feature is optional, but is encouraged to best highlight your practice and ensure your information is up-to-date.

## Enhanced business listings management

Our team will ensure that your practice appears in major online directories with up-to-date contact information and other pertinent business details. Proactive management of your listings will also help your Google search rankings.

## Review solicitation and ratings

SignatureMD offers automated review solicitation to help you better manage your online reputation and request positive reviews from patients.

## Online reputation management

This program allows SignatureMD to actively monitor your online reputation, notify you of any negative reviews and, at your request, assist with the removal of negative reviews that violate a review platform's terms of service.

## Non-member emails

SignatureMD sends emails to your non-member patients about the benefits of membership-based medicine to encourage them to become a member of your Personalized Care Program.

## Healthy Living electronic patient newsletter

Healthy Living, SignatureMD's electronic patient newsletter, is curated each month with engaging, relevant content, healthy tips and recipes, and wellness information that's delivered directly to their inbox.

## Ongoing membership materials and collateral

As part of our commitment to helping you attain long-term success, our marketing experts continue to work on your behalf, even after your practice conversion is complete. We provide a professionally designed package of patient-facing collateral.

## Paid search &amp; display advertising

SignatureMD will include your practice in our paid search program to drive new patient leads to your SignatureMD website.

To learn more about these initiatives and to sign up, contact the Physician Engagement Team at physicians@signaturemd.com
