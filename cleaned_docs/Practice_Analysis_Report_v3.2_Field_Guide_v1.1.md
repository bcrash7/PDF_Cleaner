PRACTICE ANALYSIS REPORT – FIELD GUIDE

**Panel Details**

**Total panel:** All active patients, even those we cannot market to or convert. This is useful to confirm for the doctor (usually a familiar number) and useful for us that we captured everyone.

**Conversion panel:** Excludes Medicaid, capitated, and any other patients who will not be engaged during conversion, but includes every patient who will be a prospective member during conversion (patients under 40, single-visit patients, specialist visits, patients from ancillary business lines like Medspa, Medical Weight Loss, Hormone Replacement, and sometimes payer-specific scenarios where patients are excluded from the analysis due to likelihood but have the option to join a membership model).

Average Conversion Panel Size: 2757 (completed conversions, previous 3 years)

**Analysis panel:** Varies based on the practice, location and specifics but always excludes: Medicaid, Inactive/Deceased Patients, Single Visit patients, patients with missing addresses. Patients under 40 are included in counts but have zero value in the analysis estimate. HMO/Advantage Plans are typically excluded. Visit types, extender visits, and other practice-specific scenarios are sometimes excluded, depending on practice specifics and concierge model.

Average Analysis Panel Size: 2389 (completed conversions, previous 3 years)

**Estimate &amp; Financials**

**Estimated Concierge Patients:** Typically, our expected outcome +/- 10% based on past conversions with similar panel metrics. Larger panels and unique panels (specialists, multiple locations, newer practices, etc.) may have a larger range.

**Membership Income Estimate:** Expected members x membership fee minus SMD Fee (100 members x 2,100 = $210,000 – 33% = $140,700).

**Insurance Reimbursement Estimate:** Based on low/high member counts x expected number of annual visits x average allowed charge for an established patient/moderate complexity office visit (99214) (Current formula - 3.5 visits/year x $150/visit = $525/member. 100 members = $52,500/year).

**Total Estimated Membership Income:** Membership Income Estimate + Insurance Reimbursement Estimate

**Patient Segmentation:**

The categories are based on 72 consumer data metrics (currently and growing), along with engineered persona data and tied closely to our historical conversion rates for unique population subsets.

**Proactive (Top 4 Mosaics) – 18% Conversion Rate:** Individuals in this segment are affluent, educated and not constrained by financial limitations. They represent less than 10% of the US population but account for a large portion of concierge practice members.

**Healthcare Focused (Mosaics 5-12) – 11% Conversion Rate:** Patients in this segment are typically older and have established healthcare needs they are motivated to prioritize. This group represents the largest segment of the US population. They are financially stable and often empty-nesters who appreciate personalized and attentive care.

**Value Based (Mosaics 13-23) – 8% Conversion Rate:** This segment represents a demographically diverse portion of the population in terms of age, lifestyle and where they live (mixed between urban, suburban and rural areas). While not as affluent as the previous two segments, they can afford concierge services and care about quality healthcare but tend to prioritize value over other concerns.

**On The Fence (Mosaics 24-37) – 5% Conversion Rate:** This group is also diverse in several ways (age, priorities, marital status, and income), but they tend to live closer to cities, many are single or just starting families, and for some they must balance their concern for quality healthcare with affordability.

**Low Probability (Mosaics 38-72) – 3% Conversion Rate:** This segment is unlikely to join a Concierge practice because they are not likely to prioritize healthcare due to age, family situation, or because the cost is prohibitive.
