# Who is SignatureMD

Through concierge medicine, SignatureMD creates doctor-patient partnerships that are based on extraordinary understanding and trust. Our model enables patients to enter into a high-availability, one-on-one, long-term relationship with their trusted physician. They will not only find it easy to schedule an appointment the very same or next business day, they also can expect that their appointment will start on time and continue for as long as it takes to deliver the kind of genuine, thoughtful care they deserve.

This personalized approach to health and well-being is something that SignatureMD has been perfecting for more than 13 years, in 35 states, and with more than 200 physicians. It’s also why our affilates and their patients should consider joining SignatureMD member.
